About Me

My name is Tanner White and I am origionally from Kansas City. I moved to Denver, Colorado after graduating from high school to attend the University of Denver. I recieved a BSBA with a focus in managment in 2008.


After finishing college I spend the next 9 years owning and operating a Franchise of College Hunks Hauling Junk which is a junk removal and moving company. Overtime my passion for the business dwindled and I eventually sold it is 2017 to puruse the exciting world of coding.


So far I am loving the challenge that coding presents and am looking forward to learning and purusing a career in coding and web development.
